Early Passion for Designing & Managing

After seeing how drawing & designing using softwares like AutoCAD can impact how things can be efficiently and effectively manufactured, I self-taught myself in learning the modelling of objects in order to solve production issues and prevent future problems using the data gathered from the model drawing generated. From here, I expanded my passion in drawing to estimates involving building utilities fabrication and construction.

Focus on Auxiliary Systems Design

Following the construction trend of the 2010's in our City, also the rise of engineers that will are capable to design & draw plans. This is when I shifted focus and left the manufacturing industry for the construction industry. When construction works rise, also the Auxiliary Systems demand. It was in my second company that my skills in drwaing was very useful in the creating of floor plans and using it for BOM & Proposal Plans.

Start of Managing Projects

Having able to understand how projects are planned and quoted, I decided to give a shot to start managing small projects at the early stages. After proving my capacity to effectively implement small-medium sized projects, it was not long after that I was tasked to handle large projects. Then on, I was handling the Surveys, BOM Creating, Implementation, Resource Planning, up to Project Hand-over Phases.

Continuing Professional Development

I am committed to leveraging my skills and expertise to contribute to meaningful projects that have a positive impact on company goals, so much more on society.
